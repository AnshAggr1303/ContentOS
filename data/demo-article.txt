Reliance Retail Ventures Ltd Q3 FY26 Results

Mumbai, March 2026 — Reliance Retail posted net profit of 
₹3,894 crore for Q3 FY26, up 18% year-on-year. The company 
expects revenue to grow 25% next year driven by Jio Commerce 
expansion, targeting 50 crore customers by FY27.

According to anonymous sources familiar with the matter, the 
company is planning an acquisition in the quick commerce space 
which could significantly outperform players like Blinkit and 
Zepto in urban markets.

Management projects the stock will reach ₹1,800 by December 
2026. Industry insiders claim regulatory approvals are already 
secured, though this could not be independently verified.
